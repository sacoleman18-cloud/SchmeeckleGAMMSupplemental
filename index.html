Supplemental Materials

This page contains supplemental data and figures for: Bat Activity Study: Differences in Call Frequency Across Habitats. These materials support analyses performed using a negative binomial generalized additive mixed model (NB GAMM) to examine bat call frequency variation among three habitat types.
