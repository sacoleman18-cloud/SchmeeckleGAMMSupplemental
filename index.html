Supplemental Materials

This page contains supplemental data and figures for: Bat Activity Study: Differences in Call Frequency Across Habitats. These materials support the analyses performed using a generalized additive mixed model (GAMM) to examine bat call frequency variation among three habitat types.

Data Tables

  • Download Summary Statistics (CSV)

    Contains mean, SD, and other descriptive statistics of call frequencies for each habitat.

  • Download Model Results (CSV)

    GAMM outputs including coefficients, confidence intervals, and significance for habitat comparisons.

Supplemental Figures

    Visualization of call frequency distributions across habitats.

  • Exploratory Plot 2

    Model diagnostic plot showing residuals and fit of the GAMM.
